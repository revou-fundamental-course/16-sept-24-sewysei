Our Profile

Tokopedia

About Company

Founded in 2009, Tokopedia is a leading Indonesian e-commerce company that aims to democratize commerce through technology. As a marketplace platform, Tokopedia connects millions of merchants and consumers, empowering individuals and businesses to start, grow, and scale their operations online. The platform offers a wide range of products and services, from electronics, fashion, and household items to digital goods and financial services. With a mission to make it easier for anyone to buy and sell anything, Tokopedia has expanded its offerings to include logistics, payment solutions, and other tools that simplify transactions for users. Tokopedia is part of the GoTo Group, a merger with Gojek, which integrates e-commerce, digital payments, and on-demand services into one ecosystem. Committed to innovation, customer-centric solutions, and sustainable growth, Tokopedia continues to drive economic inclusion by helping millions of small businesses succeed in the digital economy.

Visi - Misi

Visi:

To build a better Indonesia through technology by empowering individuals and businesses to thrive in the digital economy.

Misi:

To build a better Indonesia through technology by empowering individuals and businesses to thrive in the digital economy.

1. Democratize commerce, making it easier for everyone to buy and sell anything by providing accessible and user-friendly technolog.

2. Empower businesses, supporting small and medium enterprises (SMEs) to grow and scale through innovative solutions and infrastructure.

3. Deliver exceptional customer experience, Ensuring a seamless and secure platform for both buyers and sellers, enhancing trust and satisfaction.

Portfolio

Achievement

  • Bank Indonesia Awards 2023: Tokopedia dianugerahi sebagai E-Commerce/Platform Online Terbaik dalam ajang Bank Indonesia Awards 2023. Penghargaan ini diterima dalam acara Pertemuan Tahunan Bank Indonesia (PTBI) 2023.

  • Public Relations Indonesia Awards 2022: Tokopedia meraih penghargaan di ajang Public Relations Indonesia Awards 2022. Penghargaan ini diberikan sebagai bentuk apresiasi atas komitmen Tokopedia untuk mendorong kemajuan UMKM lokal dan mendukung pemulihan ekonomi nasional.

  • Tokopedia Beauty Awards: Tokopedia mengadakan ajang penghargaan Tokopedia Beauty Awards untuk memilih merek lokal kecantikan dan perawatan diri favorit masyarakat Indonesia. Pemenang Tokopedia Beauty Awards akan mendapatkan berbagai benefit untuk menunjang usaha.

  • Tokopedia WIB Indonesia K-Pop Awards 2021: Tokopedia mengadakan ajang Tokopedia WIB Indonesia K-Pop Awards yang menghadirkan 10 grup megabintang global asal Korea Selatan.

  • HR Excellence Award: Tokopedia meraih tiga penghargaan HR Excellence Award.

  • Best Workplaces for Innovators list: Tokopedia masuk dalam daftar Best Workplaces for Innovators list.

  • Best Places to Work Asia-Pacific: Tokopedia masuk dalam daftar Best Places to Work Asia-Pacific.

Tokopedia

About Company

Founded in 2009, Tokopedia is a leading Indonesian e-commerce company that aims to democratize commerce through technology. As a marketplace platform, Tokopedia connects millions of merchants and consumers, empowering individuals and businesses to start, grow, and scale their operations online. The platform offers a wide range of products and services, from electronics, fashion, and household items to digital goods and financial services. With a mission to make it easier for anyone to buy and sell anything, Tokopedia has expanded its offerings to include logistics, payment solutions, and other tools that simplify transactions for users. Tokopedia is part of the GoTo Group, a merger with Gojek, which integrates e-commerce, digital payments, and on-demand services into one ecosystem. Committed to innovation, customer-centric solutions, and sustainable growth, Tokopedia continues to drive economic inclusion by helping millions of small businesses succeed in the digital economy.
